Privacy

Website privacy notice

How information submitted through this website is handled.

Information collected

Howard Dynamics may collect the information you submit through contact and career-interest forms, along with basic technical information such as IP address, browser user agent, and submission time.

How information is used

Submitted information may be used to respond to inquiries, evaluate business or teaming opportunities, maintain a candidate pipeline, prevent abuse, and improve website operations.

Do not submit sensitive information

Do not submit classified information, controlled unclassified information, export-controlled technical data, Social Security numbers, financial account information, medical information, passwords, or copies of identity documents.

Retention and sharing

Information may be retained for legitimate business, recruiting, compliance, and security purposes. It is not sold. It may be shared with service providers or business partners only when reasonably necessary to evaluate or perform relevant work.

Contact

Use the contact form to request access, correction, or deletion of information previously submitted through the website.
