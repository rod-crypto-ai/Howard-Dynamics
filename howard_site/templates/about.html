About Howard Dynamics

Built around one mission: dependable operations.

Established in 2017 and headquartered in Texarkana, Texas, Howard Dynamics provides focused operations and maintenance support for government, defense, prime-contractor, and commercial requirements.

Company position

O&M is the business—not one item buried in a long service list.

Howard Dynamics is structured around fleet and equipment maintenance, facility and site operations, logistics and material support, technical field services, and workforce readiness. Those are not unrelated offerings; they are connected parts of the same operational system.

The company supports direct government needs and defined subcontracting workshare. Howard Dynamics does not claim every requirement is a fit. Scope, access, staffing, supply, schedule, and performance risk are evaluated before a commitment is made.
